    • About APTE

    Asia-Pacific Teacher Exchange for Global Education (APTE) is a bilateral teacher exchange programme between the Republic of Korea and Asia-Pacific partner countries, hosted by the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Korea and implemented by the Asia-Pacific Centre of Education for International Understanding under the auspices of UNESCO (APCEIU)

    In collaboration with the Ministries of Education of the partner countries. Launched with two partner countries Mongolia and the Philippines in 2012, APTE has grown into a well-known regional programme, now working with seven partner countries in the Asia-Pacific region: Cambodia, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mongolia, Philippines, Thailand, and Vietnam.
