    The SSAEM Conference acted as a platform to share results of the APTE Programme and was significant as Korean teachers and teachers from partner countries presented their experience of the online teaching exchange in the midst of the pandemic. Furthermore, the conference played an essential role as delegates of the Ministry of Education from eight participating countries (Republic of Korea, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Mongolia, the Philippines, and Thailand) discussed the education in Asia Pasific.
