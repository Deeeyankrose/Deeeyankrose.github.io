    Train to Busan

    Gamcheon Culture Village are well known as Santorini of Korea and also Korea's Machu Pichu. Tourist can enjoy beautiful art and mural in this village. You can travel by KTX (Express train) from Seoul to Busan.

    Library Mall

    The spectacular Starfield Library in Coex Mall, Seoul city was a sensation from the moment it opened. Its dramatic design and unexpected location in the middle of a huge mall attracted bibliophiles, selfie-takers and tourists from all over the globe.

    New Capital

    Sejong Lake Park is a large scale man-made park and is one of the major tourism destinations in Sejong-si. And Sejong was founded in 2007 as the new planned capital of South Korea.

    Shalat

    The Seoul Central Mosque was established to be a place to worship for Muslims in Itaewon. It is open for anyone can and outside there are many shops that Muslims and the general public can conveniently visit, consisting of halal restaurants, Islamic books, halal supermarkets, and travel agencies.

    Hanbok

    Changdeokgung Palace is of one-minute walking distance from Changdeokgung Palace, the only Korean ancient palace that was selected as World Heritage. You can travelling around while having Hanbok experience. And it is near with the Bukchon Hanok Village.

    Music Bank

    If you enjoyed Korean Idol then you should watch Music Bank Show in KBS TV Station. Located in Seoul you can watch free tv shoot in inside and outside studio and maybe you'll able to meet some idol.
